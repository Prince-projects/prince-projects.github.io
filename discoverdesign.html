Discover Design

This project was created in collaboration with other visual communications students. For this project, 8 others and myself had to create commercial materials for the university of canberra’s second year showcase, this would showcase students' works from many design degrees. For this project, I designed the logo and the icon, while other materials were designed by other students. Here you can see how I would have designed posters, brochures and other commercial material for this project. You can also find my work posted on the University of Canberra’s Visual Communications Instagram page.
