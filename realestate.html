Calgary Homes With Heart

For this project, I was offered an opportunity to work with a real estate agent and create a logo that would be placed on all of her commercial materials (brochures, website, instagram posts, etc.). Her main goal is to give people “Homes with Heart” so having that as a part of the logo was a big deal to her, I created many different ideations of the logo and in the end, we stuck with something simple but still conveys the message she wants in a modern, sweet logo that convey her personality and the homes she sells.
