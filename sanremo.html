San Remo Packaging

This project was created during my degree and during covid, the idea of this project was to create a more sustainable package that still follows the company's main design goals while bettering the company’s carbon footprint. I created a new package to hold San Remo’s angel hair pasta noodles and the display packaging for when people want to grab the pasta off the shelf at the store.
